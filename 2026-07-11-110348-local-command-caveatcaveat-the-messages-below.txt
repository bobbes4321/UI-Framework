в—Ҹ TOC = Table of Contents вҖ” a single document that lists and links to all your
  other docs, organized hierarchically. Think of it like a book's contents page.

  Example shape:

  - [Quick start (5 min)](./quick-start.md)
  - [Authoring guide (mental model)](./authoring-guide.md)
